The Best Birthday

August felt like a tale of two halves. While we spent the first half apart because of your incredible Europe trip, the second half of the month was a whirlwind of fun and intimacy together. You consistently show up for me in the most monumental moments of my life, and my 21st birthday was no difference. The effort you put in to constantly make me happy is remarkable. Having fun with my friends in the basement, breaking the t break, and meandering through New York City with you made me the happiest I’ve ever been. That alone would’ve been all I could’ve asked for out of a birthday, but you didn’t stop there. Before Le Pavillon, michelin star restaurants were only something I had heard about and watched from the countless Youtube cooking and restaurant videos I used to and still do binge. I thought it would be years before I actually had the chance to dine in one. You unveiled this dream and fantasy that had been building in my mind for years, and executed it to perfection. The fact that you picked a perfect restaurant made the already perfect day the hands down best day of my life. All because of you.
